Experience

This page contains my experiences.

Hiking and picnic trip to the Mat Cincang Mountain

I hiked with my friends, April 7, 2017

Before MCO (Movement Control Order), I loved to do extreme sport activities. One of it is hiking. There are several times when me and my friends go on a hiking plus picnic trip to the peak of Mat Cincang Mountain. The journey are tiresome but still it is worthwhile.

Becoming a promoter of wedding’s theme settings

I took a lot of weddings' ceremony throne and theme pictures, Mac 20, 2015

In the early days of my high school life I loved to take picture of every weddings ceremony throne and theme. Until I was recommended to become a promoter of wedding’s theme settings. I can give the best option and recommendation to the customer base on budget and location. It's not that long just for a few months I entered the year of Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia (SPM). To be in a focus state I lay low for a while and maybe one day get back into the field.

Taking part in marching competition

I took part with my squad, June 7, 2016

In my senior year me and my squad were chosen to represent Kedah for a marching competition. The competition were held at Dataran Merdeka. After 3 months of drills and trainings, my squad finally ready mentally and physically. The competition was attended by other contingents from other states. I still remembered the hot scorching day without any clouds to shade us. We were helpless but for the determining though of victory, we stood still and praise to Allah. That day we won and to also be mentioned the best contingent in Malaysia’s history.
